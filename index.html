My journey in tech is fueled by my passion for learning, coding, and being part of a community that contributes to career growth. So, I choose Altschool to fulfil my long-lived dream. I gained admission in the Tinyuka 2023 session but deferred to the Karatu session. I aspire to graduate as a seasoned and professional Front-end developer and use my skills to solve problems in the software development industry. My goals for AltSchool is to learn both soft and technical skills, make friends, network, be a part of a community, meet people who inspire me in my tech journey, and lastly, be prepared for the world of technology.
